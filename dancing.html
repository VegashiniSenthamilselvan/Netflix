Dancing for the Devil

"Dancing with the Devil" is a documentary series that premiered on YouTube in March 2021. It provides an intimate and candid look into the life of singer-songwriter Demi Lovato. The series is divided into four parts, each exploring different aspects of Demi Lovato's personal and professional journey, including her struggles with addiction, mental health issues, and recovery.

"Dancing with the Devil" is a four-part documentary series that provides an intimate and revealing look into the life of Demi Lovato, an American singer, songwriter, and actress. Reclaiming PowerIn the third part, "Dancing with the Devil" focuses on Demi Lovato's journey towards recovery and sobriety. RebirthThe final part of the documentary series explores Demi Lovato's ongoing healing process and her efforts to find balance and happiness.
